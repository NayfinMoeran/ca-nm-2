Carnival Attractions

Ferris Wheel Of Flames

  • A ferris wheel that burns continuously.
  • When a carriage reaches the top, it stops burning for a second, allowing the person a moment of rest
  • It keeps rotating continuously, and the person has to escape
  • The flames will not burn, but they are very hot, and cause hallucinations of the persons fears.

Knife Throwing Wheel

  • You enter it with two people.
  • One person gets on the wheel, another has to throw the knives.
  • They have to throw at least 5 knives, and the max is 10.
  • When the person gets hit, they lose a month off their life.

Animal Show

  • You enter it by yourself.
  • You must face off against twisted, spooky animals in an arena.
  • You have to defeat 3 animals.
  • You can only leave if you win, or die. There's no loopholes.
